<b>Comm Operator Commands</b>

All Regular User and Operator commands, plus the following:


/read			when comm Ops type read they will be able to delete specific messages
/clear -		clears the whole room
/clearbuffer		check all cleared messages that day
/deop [nick]		deactivates a user's Operator status
/deoplist		displays a list of deoped Operators
/reop [nick]		reactivates a user's Operator status
/clear [text]		Comm ops will silently clear from the main text matching word
/comm           	THE MOST IMPORTANT COMM COMMAND, you will get all important comm links
/id [nick]		id for comm ops is MORE advanced, it will give you all available information about that [nick]
